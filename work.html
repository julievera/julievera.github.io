Strategic Work

Domains of practice, not a project archive. Detailed process, methods, and findings are reserved for interviews.

AI-generated content and trust calibration I help teams understand where AI-authored content improves decision-making, where it introduces doubt, and what kinds of claims require proof, provenance, or human accountability. Recent work includes research on AI-generated vehicle summaries in a high-stakes automotive purchase journey.
Research infrastructure for ambiguous product spaces I build research programs where teams lack shared vocabulary, foundational knowledge, or clear decision criteria. Recent work includes building behavior-first consumer research infrastructure for automotive retail.
Search, marketplace relevance, and decision support I study how people evaluate results, credibility signals, and choice architecture in marketplace environments. Recent work includes search relevance and job-seeker decision-making research at Monster.
Product bets and concept risk I help teams understand whether an interaction model fits a user's actual decision context before investing in build. Recent work includes evaluating swipe-based job application concepts and redirecting product attention toward higher-value needs.
Crisis information and platform-mediated expertise I study how publics assess credibility and expertise when creators, institutions, platforms, and real-time risk collide. Recent work includes doctoral research on severe weather livestreams and weatherfluencers.
Enterprise research and internal tooling Research on internal sales tools and workflow systems (Amazon, AWS), translating field evidence into product investment decisions and roadmap prioritization.
