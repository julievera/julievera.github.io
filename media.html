2023 Journal of Social Media Studies

Facts, Frames, and (Mis)interpretations: Understanding Rumors as Collective Sensemaking

This publication explores how social media users collectively make sense of ambiguous information during crisis events. Through analysis of rumor propagation patterns, we identify how framing and interpretative contexts shape the way misinformation evolves and persists within online communities.
